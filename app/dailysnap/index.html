DailySnap

Your AI-Powered Daily Diary

Stop staring at a blank page. DailySnap auto-fills your date, weather, and location — you just log what happened. Write a quick moment, snap a photo, or track your mood, and let AI turn it all into a beautifully written diary entry at the end of the day.

Key Features

AI Diary Generator

Transform your raw notes, meals, mood, and daily moments into a beautifully written first-person diary entry in seconds. Edit and tweak the output anytime.

Moment Tracking

Log unlimited moments throughout the day with mood, text notes, and photo attachments. Swipe to delete any moment you no longer need.

Mood Calendar

See your entire month at a glance with mood-colored indicators. Tap any day to read your full diary entry with all moments and photos.

Auto-Fill

Weather, date, day of week, and location fill in automatically. Less typing, more living. Everything you need is in one screen.

Cloud Backup

Back up your entire diary to your own Google Drive. Restore it on a new device anytime. Free for everyone, no server needed.

PDF Export

Export any day's diary as a beautifully formatted PDF with your diary text, moments, photos, and daily details. Share it or save it.
