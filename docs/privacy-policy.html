🎯 TL;DR: GetTaller does NOT collect personal data, does NOT use cloud storage, and does NOT track you across other apps. All your health data stays 100% on your device.

1. Introduction

GetTaller ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, GetTaller (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App. By accessing and using GetTaller, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

🚫 No Personal Account Information

GetTaller does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or submit any personally identifiable information (PII). The app operates entirely anonymously with no login required.

🚫 No Cloud Storage of Health Data

All your measurements, exercise logs, meal logs, sleep records, and personal health data are stored exclusively on your device using local encryption. We do not transmit, sync, or store this data on any cloud server or external system.

🚫 No Behavioral Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, cross-app identifiers (except as required by AdMob), or behavioral analytics to follow your activity across other applications or websites. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third-party advertisers for targeting purposes.

🚫 No Location Data

GetTaller does not request, collect, or access your device's location information.

🚫 No Contact Information

We do not access your contacts, phone number, calendar, or any contact-related data.

3. Information We DO Collect

We collect minimal, non-identifying information necessary for the app to function and improve your experience.

A. Health & Usage Data (Stored Locally)

  • Height Measurements: Current height, predicted height, daily progress
  • Personal Profile: Age, gender, birth date (used for calculation purposes only)
  • Exercise Data: Completed workouts, exercise duration, preferences
  • Nutrition Data: Logged meals and dietary information
  • Sleep Data: Logged sleep hours
  • App Settings: Theme preference, unit system (cm vs inches), language

Storage: All of the above is stored locally on your device using Hive (encrypted local database). This data is never sent to our servers.

B. Analytics Data (Non-Identifying)

We use Firebase Analytics (operated by Google) to understand how our app is used and improve features. The analytics we collect include:

  • App launch and screen view events
  • Feature usage (onboarding completion, workout started/completed, AI coach queries)
  • Ad impressions and clicks (for monetization transparency)
  • App errors and crash data

Important: Firebase does NOT collect personally identifiable information. You cannot be identified based on this data. If you prefer to opt out of analytics, you can disable data collection in your device settings (see Section 7).

C. Advertising ID (Ad Serving)

To serve non-personalized ads, we use Google Mobile Ads, which requires access to your device's Advertising ID. This identifier allows AdMob to serve ads but does not identify you personally and is not used for behavioral targeting.

D. Crash Reporting & Diagnostics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash logs and technical diagnostics when the app encounters an error. This helps us improve app stability. These logs do not contain your personal health data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To calculate height predictions, provide personalized exercise recommendations, and deliver AI coaching
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand user behavior and improve app features, performance, and user experience
  • Crash Diagnostics: To identify and fix technical issues
  • Ad Serving: To deliver non-personalized advertisements to support our free model
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes

5. Third-Party Services

GetTaller integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy practices:

A. Firebase (Google)

  • Services Used: Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Functions
  • Data Shared: Analytics events (non-identifying), crash logs
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

B. Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

  • Services Used: Non-personalized ad serving (banner, interstitial, rewarded, native ads)
  • Data Shared: Advertising ID, non-personalized impression events
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Here's how we protect your information:

  • Local Encryption: Health data stored on your device is encrypted using Hive's encryption capabilities
  • Transport Security: All communication with our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • No Unnecessary Data: We minimize data collection to reduce security risks
  • No Cloud Backups: Your health data is not synced to cloud storage, eliminating cloud-related breach risks

Important: While we implement security measures, no system is 100% secure. If you sync your device to cloud services (iCloud, Google Drive), your local device backup may be encrypted through those services. We recommend you manage your device backup settings according to your comfort level.

7. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

A. Data Access & Deletion

Since all health data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Uninstalling GetTaller will remove all app-specific data from your device
  • Manual Deletion: You can delete individual entries through the app settings

B. Analytics Opt-Out

To disable Firebase Analytics:

  • iOS: Settings → GetTaller → Turn off analytics
  • Android: Settings → Apps → GetTaller → Permissions → Disable analytics (if available)

C. Advertising Opt-Out

To opt out of personalized ads (GetTaller already uses non-personalized ads, but you can further restrict):

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (toggle off)
  • Android: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad personalization (toggle off)

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

GetTaller is designed for users of all ages, including children under 13. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We use non-personalized ads for all users, including children
  • All analytics are aggregated and non-identifying
  • Parents/guardians have full control over the device and data on it

If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

  • Local App Data: Retained on your device until you uninstall the app or manually delete entries
  • Firebase Analytics: Retained for up to 14 months by Google
  • Crash Logs: Retained for debugging purposes; typically deleted after analysis (within 90 days)
  • Advertising ID: Retained as long as you use the app; resets when you reset your device's ad ID
