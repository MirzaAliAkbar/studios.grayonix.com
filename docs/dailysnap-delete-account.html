DailySnap stores ALL your diary data locally on your device. We never upload your diary entries or moments to our servers. Account deletion removes your login credentials and any cloud backup from your Google Drive.

How to Delete Your Account

To request permanent deletion of your DailySnap account and associated data, follow these steps:

1 Open DailySnap and go to Profile

Open the app, tap the Profile icon in the top-right corner, then tap Log Out.

2 Send us an email

Email us at AliAkbar@tutamail.com with the subject line: "Delete My DailySnap Account"

Include the email address you used to sign in to DailySnap (Google account or email/password).

Send Deletion Request Email

3 Wait for confirmation

We will process your request and send you a confirmation email within 7 business days. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted at that time.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Firebase Auth Account: Your login credentials (Google Sign-In or email/password)
  • User Profile: Your display name, email, city, and country stored in Firestore
  • Cloud Backup: Any DailySnap backup file stored in your Google Drive's hidden app-data folder (we delete it from our side; you can also delete it manually from your Drive)
  • Premium Subscription: Any active premium status is revoked

What Stays on Your Device

IMPORTANT: Your diary data (entries, moments, photos, mood data) is stored locally on YOUR device — not on our servers. We cannot access or delete it remotely.

After account deletion, your local diary data remains on your device until you:

  • Uninstall DailySnap — this removes all app data from your device
  • Delete individual moments — swipe left on any moment in the timeline to remove it

Since your data is entirely local, no third party (including us) has access to it after account deletion.

Retention Period

  • Account deletion: Processed within 7 business days of your email request
  • Firebase user record: Deleted immediately upon processing
  • Firestore user profile: Deleted immediately upon processing
  • Cloud backup: Deleted from Google Drive immediately upon processing (you can also delete it yourself from your Drive at any time)
