TL;DR: DailySnap stores ALL your diary data locally on your device. We never upload your entries to our servers. Cloud backups go to YOUR Google Drive — only you can access them.

1. Introduction

DailySnap ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, DailySnap (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App. By accessing and using DailySnap, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

No Diary Content on Our Servers

Your diary entries, moments, photos, mood data, and personal reflections are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive). We do not transmit, sync, or store this data on any cloud server controlled by us.

No Behavioral Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies, cross-app identifiers (except as required by AdMob), or behavioral analytics to follow your activity across other applications or websites. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third-party advertisers for targeting purposes.

No Location Tracking

DailySnap displays your approximate city-level weather and location for diary context, but we do not track, store, or transmit your precise GPS location to any server.

3. Information We DO Collect

We collect minimal, non-identifying information necessary for the app to function and improve your experience.

A. Diary Data (Stored Locally on Your Device)

  • Diary Entries: AI-generated diary text, daily notes, free-text thoughts
  • Moments: Text notes, mood selections, timestamps, photo attachments
  • Daily Tracking: Meals, exercise, sleep, work hours, study hours, water intake, people met
  • Mood & Ratings: Daily mood (1-5) and day rating (1-5)
  • Photos: Images attached to moments, stored in your device's app directory
  • Weather & Location: City-level weather and location used for auto-fill (not tracked)

Storage: All of the above is stored locally on your device using Hive (local database). This data is never sent to our servers.

B. Google Account (Authentication Only)

If you sign in with Google, we store your Google User ID, display name, and email address in Firebase Firestore solely for account identification. We do not access your Google Drive, contacts, or any other Google services beyond what you explicitly authorize for backup purposes.

C. Cloud Backup (Your Google Drive)

If you choose to use the Cloud Backup feature, your diary data is packaged into a zip file and uploaded to your own Google Drive using the hidden app-data folder. We never have access to this file — it lives entirely in your Google account. You can delete it at any time from your Drive.

D. Advertising ID (Ad Serving)

To serve non-personalized ads, we use Google Mobile Ads, which requires access to your device's Advertising ID. This identifier allows AdMob to serve ads but does not identify you personally and is not used for behavioral targeting.

E. AI Diary Generation

When you tap "Generate Diary," your logged data (meals, mood, moments, notes) is sent to the DeepSeek AI API to generate a diary entry. This data is processed temporarily to generate the text and is not stored by the AI service beyond the processing request. The generated diary is then saved locally on your device.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To generate AI diary entries, display your timeline, and render your calendar
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand user behavior and improve app features
  • Crash Diagnostics: To identify and fix technical issues
  • Ad Serving: To deliver non-personalized advertisements to support our free model
  • Account Management: To identify your account and manage your premium subscription

5. Third-Party Services

DailySnap integrates with the following third-party services:

A. Firebase (Google)

  • Services Used: Authentication (Google Sign-In, Email/Password), user profile storage
  • Data Shared: User ID, display name, email (account info only)
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

B. Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

  • Services Used: Non-personalized ad serving (banner and rewarded ads)
  • Data Shared: Advertising ID, non-personalized impression events
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

C. DeepSeek AI API

  • Service Used: AI diary generation from your logged data
  • Data Shared: Temporary diary data (meals, mood, moments, notes) for text generation only
  • Storage: Data is not stored beyond the generation request

D. Google Drive API

  • Service Used: Backup and restore of your diary data to your own Google Drive
  • Data Shared: Zipped diary archive uploaded to your hidden app-data folder
  • Access: We only request the drive.appdata scope — we cannot read your other Drive files

6. Data Security

  • Local Storage: All diary data is stored locally on your device
  • Transport Security: All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • No Cloud Servers: Your diary content is never stored on our servers
  • Your Drive, Your Data: Backups live in your Google Drive — we have zero access

7. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

A. Data Access & Deletion

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Uninstalling DailySnap will remove all app data from your device
  • Moment Deletion: You can delete individual moments by swiping left on the timeline
  • Backup Deletion: Delete the backup file from your Google Drive at any time

B. Advertising Opt-Out

  • Android: Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad personalization (toggle off)

8. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

DailySnap is designed for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children
  • All diary data is stored locally on the device
  • We use non-personalized ads for all users

9. Data Retention

  • Local Diary Data: Retained on your device until you delete entries or uninstall the app
  • Cloud Backups: Retained in your Google Drive until you delete them
  • Firebase Account Data: Retained while your account is active; deleted upon account removal
  • Advertising ID: Retained as long as you use the app; resets when you reset your device's ad ID

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the app following changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
